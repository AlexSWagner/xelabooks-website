Data & Account Deletion

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

We believe you should have full control over your data. This page outlines the process for requesting the deletion of your personal data and your Audiobook Buddy account.

How to Request Deletion

To initiate the deletion of your account and/or personal data, you must send an email to our support team. Please follow the steps below to ensure a smooth process.

Click the button below to open your email client and send a deletion request. Please include your account username or the email address associated with your account in the body of the email.

Request Data Deletion

The Deletion Process

Once we receive your request, we will first verify your identity to protect your account's security. This may involve asking you to confirm the request from the email address associated with your account.

  • Verification: We will contact you to confirm the deletion request.
  • Processing Time: Please allow up to 30 days for the deletion process to be completed.
  • Confirmation: We will notify you by email once your data has been permanently deleted from our systems.

Please note that deleting your account is irreversible and will permanently remove all your library data, listening progress, and personal settings.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please do not hesitate to contact us through the same email address: alex@xelafy.com.
