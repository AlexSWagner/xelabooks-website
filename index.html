Your ultimate audiobook companion. Import, organize, and enjoy your personal audiobook collection with powerful features and seamless cloud synchronization.

Download on Google Play

Features You'll Love

Personal Library

Import your audiobooks from Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, or local storage. Organize your collection with beautiful cover art and detailed metadata.

Cloud Sync

Your library and listening progress sync seamlessly across all your devices. Never lose your place in a book again.

Profiles & Listening Stats

Create an account to track your listening habits. See your total time listened, books completed, and other fun stats just for you.

Public Domain Library

Access thousands of free public domain audiobooks from the Internet Archive directly within the app.

Smart Playback

Advanced playback controls with variable speed, sleep timer, bookmarks, 5-band equalizer, and pitch control. Customize your listening experience.

Highly Customizable

Choose from 12 custom themes, toggle compact library view, and enjoy full multi-language support including English, German, French, Japanese, and Korean.
